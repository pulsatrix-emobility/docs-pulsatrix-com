## Reference - LED colors and charging states
### Main LED
- `Green`
  - *Static and/or slowly pulsing*
    - Charger available
  - *Blinking*
    - Authorization in progress/Authorization successful
- `Blue`
  - *Static*, darker blue
    - Connected
  - *Static* (lighter bulue) or *Flowing*
    - Charging
  - *Static* or *Pulsing*
    - Paused/Stopped
- `Red`
  - *Static*
    - Not available (firmware update, RCD test, error)
  - *Static* or *Pulsing*
    - Charging error
  - *Blinking*
    - Authorization error
- `Pink`
  - *Static* or *Pulsing*
    - Waiting for authorization
- `White`
  - *Outwards Flowing*
    - Authorization in progress
- `Turqoise`
  - *Static* or *Flowing*
    - Lingering
- `Yellow`/`Orange`
  - *Static*
    - Firmware upgrade in progress
  - *Slowly pulsing*
    - Error
